How Brands Appear in AI-Generated Answers & Recommendations

AI systems are increasingly shaping how users discover products and companies. Rather than browsing traditional search results, users now rely on AI-generated answers and recommendation systems. This thesis investigates which brands become visible in AI outputs, how digital authority and online presence drive that visibility, and whether it affects brand perception, trust, and purchasing decisions. The research design is adaptable to align with a partner company's strategic interests.

RQ 1

How visible are different companies in AI-generated answers, and how can this visibility be measured?

RQ 2

Which companies are recommended more frequently by AI systems within the same category, and why?

RQ 3

How do content structure, digital presence, and positioning influence AI visibility?

RQ 4

Does higher AI visibility influence brand perception or customer preference?

Methodology AI Query Experiments, Competitive Visibility Analysis, Digital Presence Analysis, User Perception Survey
Frameworks GEO, LLMO, AI-Driven Brand Visibility
Tools ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Semrush/Ahrefs
